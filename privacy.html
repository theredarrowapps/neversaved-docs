Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

This Privacy Policy explains how NeverSaved ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Our Commitment to Privacy

NeverSaved is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers. The App operates entirely on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do not store phone numbers you enter
  • We do not store messages you compose
  • We do not collect your contact list
  • We do not track your messaging activity
  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not access your location data

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

User Preferences

  • Your dark mode preference
  • Your selected language preference
  • Your last selected country code
  • App usage count for ad display timing

This information remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Clipboard Access

The App can detect when you have copied a phone number to your clipboard. This feature:

  • Only activates when you open the App
  • Only reads numeric content from your clipboard
  • Does not store clipboard content
  • Does not transmit clipboard content to any server
  • Is used solely to provide convenience in entering phone numbers

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription management and in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • Purchase transaction data
  • Device information

RevenueCat's privacy practices are described in their Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

External Messaging Applications

When you use NeverSaved to open a chat in external messaging applications (WhatsApp, Telegram, iMessage, Signal, Viber), you are subject to those applications' privacy policies. We do not control or have access to any data shared with these applications.

How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used to:

  • Enable the core functionality of opening chat windows in messaging apps
  • Remember your app preferences (language, theme, country code)
  • Display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • Manage subscriptions and in-app purchases through RevenueCat
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • With Google AdMob for advertisement display (as described above)
  • With RevenueCat for subscription management (as described above)
  • With external messaging apps when you choose to open a chat (the phone number and optional message you enter)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

The App is available globally and supports 200+ countries. However, please note that third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) may transfer data to servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • You can disable clipboard access in your device settings
  • You can clear locally stored preferences by uninstalling the App
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings
  • You can disable ad tracking through your device's privacy settings
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through the App Store

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information:

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • We do not maintain servers that store user data
  • Communication with third-party services uses industry-standard encryption
  • We regularly update the App to address security vulnerabilities

However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
  • Material changes will be notified through the App or via email
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
